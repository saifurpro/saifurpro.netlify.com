What services do you provide? +
I specialize in WordPress development (including WooCommerce & Elementor/Divi), Shopify development, HTML email template design, MailChimp email marketing, and B2B Lead Generation via LinkedIn and Apollo.io.
How long does a website project take? +
A simple landing page typically takes 2–3 days. A full business website takes 5–7 days. A WooCommerce eCommerce store takes 7–14 days depending on complexity, number of products, and integrations required.
Do you offer revisions? +
Yes! All projects include at least 2 revisions. For the Professional and higher plans, I offer unlimited revisions until you're 100% satisfied. I'm very patient and always work until you love the final result.
What if I'm not satisfied with the work? +
I offer a money-back guarantee for clients who are dissatisfied with their purchases. Your satisfaction is my top priority, and I'll work with you to resolve any issues before considering a refund.
Do I need to provide content for my website? +
Ideally yes — you should provide text content, your logo, and any images you want to use. However, I can help with placeholder content and stock imagery to get you started. Copywriting is available as an add-on service.
Which page builders do you work with? +
I am highly experienced with Elementor Pro, Divi Builder, WPBakery, Gutenberg (Block Editor), PageFly (Shopify), and Shogun (Shopify). I can work with whichever builder you prefer or recommend the best one for your project.
Can you work on my existing website? +
Absolutely! I frequently work on existing WordPress, WooCommerce, and Shopify websites for redesigns, bug fixes, speed optimization, new feature additions, plugin updates, and ongoing maintenance.
How do we communicate during the project? +
I reply instantly on WhatsApp and within 2 hours on Email and LinkedIn. I typically provide progress updates after each milestone and share a staging/preview link so you can see the work in real-time.
What payment methods do you accept? +
I accept payments via Upwork, Fiverr, PayPal, Wise (TransferWise), and bank transfer. For direct clients, I typically require a 50% upfront payment and the remaining 50% upon project completion.
Do you offer post-launch support? +
Yes! I provide 7 days of free post-launch support for all projects to handle any small issues or questions. For ongoing support and maintenance, I offer monthly retainer packages — ask me for details.
Can you handle hosting and domain? +
I don't sell hosting directly, but I can guide you to set up with reliable providers like SiteGround, Cloudways, or Hostinger. I can install WordPress, configure your domain, set up SSL, and handle all the technical setup for you.
